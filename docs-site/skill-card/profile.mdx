---
title: "profile.json"
description: "Your machine-readable professional profile"
---

`profile.json` is the core of your Skill Card. It describes your professional identity in a structured format that any AI agent can read and reason over. Agents use this file to evaluate your capability match against a role.

<Note>
  Demographic fields ‚Äî age, gender, nationality, ethnicity ‚Äî are deliberately excluded from the schema to comply with the EU AI Act and to reduce bias in automated screening.
</Note>

## Schema

<ResponseField name="schema_version" type="string" required>
  Protocol version this profile conforms to. Current value: `"0.1.0"`.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="name" type="string">
  Your professional name as you want it presented to agents and employers.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="title" type="string" required>
  Your current or most recent professional title (e.g. `"Senior Software Engineer"`, `"Executive Chef"`).
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="seniority" type="string" required>
  Your seniority level. One of: `entry`, `junior`, `mid`, `senior`, `lead`, `manager`, `director`, `executive`.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="years_experience" type="integer">
  Total years of professional experience.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="availability" type="string">
  Current availability for new opportunities. One of: `immediately`, `in_2_weeks`, `in_4_weeks`, `in_8_weeks`, `not_looking`.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="primary_domains" type="string[]" required>
  Top-level professional domains you work in ‚Äî for example `Backend Engineering`, `DevOps`, or `AI/ML`. Must have at least one item. These appear in Zone 1 (public) data and are used for discovery filtering.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="skills" type="string[]" required>
  Your professional skills as a flat list. Can include coding languages, soft skills, domain knowledge, or any professional capability ‚Äî for example `"Python"`, `"Leadership"`, `"SQL"`. Must have at least one item.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="tools_and_platforms" type="string[]">
  Tools, equipment, software, or platforms used in professional work ‚Äî for example `"Kubernetes"`, `"AWS"`, `"Docker"`.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="certifications_and_licenses" type="string[]">
  Professional certifications or licenses ‚Äî for example `"AWS Solutions Architect Associate"`, `"CDL-A"`, `"ServSafe"`.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="specializations" type="string[]">
  Niche areas of deep expertise ‚Äî for example `"Event-Driven Architecture"`, `"API Gateway Design"`.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="spoken_languages" type="object[]">
  Languages you speak professionally.

  <Expandable title="item properties">
    <ResponseField name="language" type="string" required>
      Language name ‚Äî for example `"English"`, `"Mandarin"`.
    </ResponseField>

    <ResponseField name="level" type="string" required>
      Proficiency level. One of: `native`, `fluent`, `professional`, `basic`.
    </ResponseField>
  </Expandable>
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="education" type="string">
  Highest relevant education or training ‚Äî for example `"BSc Computer Science, UC Berkeley"`.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="summary" type="string">
  A 2‚Äì3 sentence professional summary. Maximum 1000 characters.
</ResponseField>

## Seniority levels

| Value | Typical profile |
|---|---|
| `entry` | 0‚Äì1 years |
| `junior` | 1‚Äì3 years |
| `mid` | 3‚Äì6 years |
| `senior` | 6‚Äì10 years |
| `lead` | 8+ years, team responsibility |
| `manager` | People management focus |
| `director` | Department or org-wide leadership |
| `executive` | C-suite or VP level |

## Privacy zones

Different fields are exposed at different access levels:

| Zone | Fields exposed | Who can access |
|---|---|---|
| **Zone 1** (public) | `title`, `seniority`, `primary_domains`, `availability` | Anyone |
| **Zone 2** (verified) | Full profile, `skills`, `years_experience`, `evidence` | Authenticated agents |
| **Zone 3** (private) | Contact details, exact salary | Only after ACCEPT |

<Tip>
  Generate your `profile.json` automatically from your CV with `scoutica scan ~/my-docs/`. The CLI pipes your documents to a local AI ‚Äî your data never leaves your machine.
</Tip>
